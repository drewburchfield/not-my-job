## Dark Pattern Warning Services

These make cancellation intentionally difficult:

### Phone-Only Cancellation
- **SiriusXM**: 1-866-635-2349 (heavy retention scripts)
- **NYTimes** (some plans): 1-800-591-9233
- **Planet Fitness**: Must go in-person

### Certified Mail Required
- **Equinox**: Certified mail to home club, 45-day notice
- **LA Fitness**: Certified mail with 30-day notice
- **Gold's Gym**: Varies by franchise

### Hidden Cancel / Retention Pressure
- **Amazon Prime**: 4+ clicks deep, multiple "are you sure"
- **Adobe Creative Cloud**: Chat required for annual plans (ETF may apply)
- **LinkedIn Premium**: Multiple guilt screens
- **SiriusXM**: Will offer 50%+ discounts - be firm

### Tips for Difficult Cancellations

**Script:** "I've already made my decision. Please process the cancellation now."

**Documentation:**
- Screenshot confirmation pages
- Save cancellation emails
- Note date/time of calls
- Get confirmation numbers

**Credit Card Backup:**
1. Request cancellation in writing (email)
2. Screenshot the request
3. If they keep charging, dispute with credit card
